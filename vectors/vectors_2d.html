Vector addition and subtraktion

\[\vec{a} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}\] \[\vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix}\] \[\vec{c} = \vec{a} + \vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix} + \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 + 1 \\ 3 + 4 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ 7 \end{pmatrix}\] \[\vec{c} = \vec{a} - \vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 - 1 \\ 3 - 4 \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\]

Vector scaling

\[\vec{a} = \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \end{pmatrix}\] We introduce a scalar called \(s\). \[s \cdot \vec{a} = s \cdot \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} s \cdot x \\ s \cdot y \end{pmatrix}\]
